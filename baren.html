Project Description

Bären Baugruppe AG is one of the major construction companies in Switzerland with many years of experience in the market. The company is well known for high-quality work and reliability in roofing, plumbing, painting, and building protection. Beyond construction, Bären Baugruppe is also a top sponsor in Swiss sports, especially football, showing its strong connection to community, teamwork, and long-term commitment.

Drawing inspiration from the powerful symbolism of the bear, this emblem embodies strength, resilience, and forward momentum. Our collaboration with BÄRENB BAUGROUPE AG was marked by a shared vision for a bold and memorable brand identity. With a focus on clean lines and modern aesthetics, we've created a logo that speaks volumes about the company's values and aspirations.
