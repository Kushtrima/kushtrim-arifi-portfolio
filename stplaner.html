Project Description

Commissioned by MONUN AG for their brand Stplanner, this project focused on a modern, minimal website expressing professionalism and trust.

I led UX research and Figma prototyping, then built the final version in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with a glass-style header, smooth transitions, and a responsive layout that embodies Stplanner's clarity and precision.

In my role as UX/UI Designer, I conducted research, created wireframes, and developed the final layout in Figma. As Front-End Developer, I hand-coded the entire structure, styling, and animations to bring the design to life with clean, maintainable code.
