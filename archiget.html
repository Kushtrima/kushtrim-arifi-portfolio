Project Description

ARCHIGET is a pioneering platform that connects future building owners with architects and construction managers. The logo design needed to convey trust, professionalism, and innovation in the Swiss construction industry.

After a call with the company, I understood they were looking for something minimal—something that represents architecture and building, but kept clean and simple. They also wanted the company name integrated directly into the logo. After working on the concepts for a few days, I presented three different options, and the company chose the one you see here as the final design.
