Project Description

Albasolar is a company based in Switzerland with operations in Kosovo. During our initial meeting, the client explained the need for a website that feels different yet remains highly functional. Since many of their investors are older, easy navigation was a top priority.

I began by clarifying the core idea of what the website should represent, then moved directly into live design sessions.

The final website includes smooth image animations, text transitions, and engaging section intros. These effects were achieved by customizing WordPress with JavaScript, CSS, and HTML blocks—without relying on plugins.

Less, but intentional.

This project follows a single rule: every element must earn its place.

Space, typography, and motion are used as functional tools—not decoration.
