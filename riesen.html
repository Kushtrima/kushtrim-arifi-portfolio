Project Description

Riesen Holzbau AG has been active in the wood industry since 1997, specializing in timber construction such as houses, cabins, and custom wooden projects. After many years with its original logo, the company decided to refresh its visual identity. Through a small design contest, my concept was selected, and since then the new logo has become a central part of the company's modern appearance.

The logo combines the outline of a house with the natural pattern of wood rings. The house shape represents construction, protection, and living spaces, while the wood texture inside symbolizes strength, durability, and craftsmanship. Together, they reflect the company's focus on building with quality, tradition, and care for lasting value.
