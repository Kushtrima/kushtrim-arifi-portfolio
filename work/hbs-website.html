Project Description

This project involved the design and development of a modern construction website commissioned by MONUN AG on behalf of their client. My role focused exclusively on UX design and front-end development, translating business requirements into a clear, functional, and visually refined digital experience.

The process began with structured UX research to understand the company's positioning, target audience, and service flow. Based on these insights, I created wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes in Figma, defining layout structure, content hierarchy, and interaction patterns before moving into implementation.

The website was then developed using WordPress, enhanced with custom JavaScript and CSS to deliver tailored interactions, performance optimizations, and a responsive layout across all devices. Particular attention was given to usability, readability, and visual consistency, ensuring the platform communicates professionalism while remaining intuitive for visitors.

Built with purpose, designed for clarity.

Every section serves a function, every interaction supports usability.

Structure, contrast, and hierarchy guide the experience, helping users find information quickly and confidently.
