Project Description

As a UX/UI Designer & Full-Stack Engineer at MONUN AG, I'm involved in every stage of product development — from research and design to implementation — ensuring both intuitive experiences and strong technical foundations for Swiss tech projects.

For this website, I handled everything from concept to launch: designing in Figma, writing the content, and developing the final product. Working closely with management, I focused on aligning user needs with business goals to deliver a solution that feels clear, functional, and true to the company's vision.

In my role as a UX and Front-End Developer, I combined user-centered design with precise implementation — transforming wireframes and prototypes into responsive, high-performance interfaces. I focused on usability, accessibility, and clean code architecture to ensure that every design decision translated effectively into a seamless digital experience.

Less, but intentional.

This project follows a single rule: every element must earn its place.

Space, typography, and motion are used as functional tools—not decoration.
